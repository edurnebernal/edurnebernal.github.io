March 30th, 2023

Una Ingeniera en cada cole 2023

Together with fellow female engineers, I been involved in this year's edition of "Una ingeniera en cada cole" ("A female engineer in every school") initiative, which aims to increase visibility and representation of women in engineering fields. Within the initiative, we visited primary schools to educate young children about the engineering field and our daily work. We talk about significant women in engineering and computer science history to inspire their interest in the field. Lastly, we did an enjoyable session on augmented reality (AR) with the children.

February 11th, 2023

International Day of Women in Science 2023

On February 11th, 2023, we celebrated the International Day of Women in Science. Julia Guerreo-Viu and I celebrated this day by giving a talk about our research at a high school. Our goal was to inspire young girls to pursue careers in STEM fields.

April 6th, 2022

Girls' Day 2022

I participated in this year's Girls' Day with my colleagues from the Graphics & Imagine Lab, which focuses on raising visibility of women in science. During the event, we introduced our research projects to high school students and let them experience interactive virtual reality applications and games. We hope that the students enjoyed their time with us and gained a better understanding of the potential of research, emphasizing that it is an inclusive place for everyone.
